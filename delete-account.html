MacroChef Account and Data Deletion

Last updated: April 10, 2026

You may request deletion of your MacroChef account and associated personal data by contacting us at:

macrocheffounder@gmail.com

Please include the email address associated with your MacroChef account.

What We Delete

When you request deletion, we will delete or anonymize account data associated with your MacroChef account where reasonably possible, including:

  • Profile information
  • Food logs
  • Weight logs
  • Saved meals
  • Favorite meals
  • Grocery items
  • Kitchen or pantry items
  • Allergy and dietary preference settings
  • Other app data associated with your account

What May Be Retained

Some information may be retained if required for legal, security, fraud prevention, billing, tax, dispute resolution, or compliance purposes.

Subscriptions

Subscription billing is handled by Apple. Deleting your MacroChef account does not automatically cancel an active Apple subscription.

You can manage or cancel subscriptions through your Apple ID subscription settings.

Verification

We may need to verify your identity before processing deletion requests.

Processing Time

We will make reasonable efforts to process deletion requests within a reasonable timeframe after verifying the request.

Contact

For account or data deletion requests, contact:

macrocheffounder@gmail.com
