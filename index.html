Privacy Policy for MacroChef

Last updated: [April,9, 2026]

MacroChef (“we,” “our,” or “us”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how MacroChef collects, uses, stores, and protects information when you use the MacroChef mobile app.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect information you provide directly in the app, including account information, profile and goal information, nutrition data, food logs, weight logs, generated meals, saved meals, grocery list items, kitchen items, allergy and dietary preferences, and photos you choose to submit for scanning or analysis.

2. Account Information

We may collect your email address, username, user ID, and authentication provider information when you create or sign into an account.

3. Health, Fitness, and Nutrition Information

MacroChef may collect information such as age, height, weight, goal weight, activity level, nutrition goals, macro targets, food logs, weight logs, and related preferences to personalize your experience.

4. Photos and Scan Data

If you use photo-based features, MacroChef may process photos you submit for food photo analysis, barcode scanning, or kitchen/fridge scanning. These photos are used to provide the requested feature, such as estimating nutrition, detecting barcode information, or suggesting visible kitchen ingredients.

5. How We Use Information

We use your information to create and manage your account, personalize calorie and macro targets, generate meal recommendations, suggest fast-food orders, analyze food and kitchen photos, maintain logs and saved meals, sync data, manage subscriptions, improve app reliability, and provide support.

6. AI Features

MacroChef uses AI-powered features to generate meals, estimate nutrition, suggest fast-food orders, identify possible kitchen ingredients, and provide related recommendations. AI results are estimates and suggestions. You should review generated meals, nutrition estimates, allergy-sensitive recommendations, and scan results before relying on them.

7. Third-Party Services

MacroChef may use third-party services including Supabase for authentication and data storage, RevenueCat for subscription management, Apple for App Store purchases and subscriptions, OpenAI or other AI providers for AI-powered features, and Open Food Facts for barcode/product lookup.

8. Subscriptions and Payments

Payments are processed by Apple through the App Store. MacroChef does not collect or store your full credit card or payment card information. We may receive subscription status information through Apple and RevenueCat.

9. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information with service providers only as needed to operate the app, provide AI features, manage subscriptions, store user data, or comply with legal obligations.

10. Data Retention

We keep your information for as long as needed to provide MacroChef services, maintain your account, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements.

11. Allergies and Health Information

MacroChef may allow you to enter allergies, dietary preferences, weight, nutrition targets, and related information. This information is used to personalize recommendations. MacroChef is not a medical app and does not provide medical advice.

12. Your Choices

You may update profile information in the app, change allergy and dietary preferences, modify certain logs and saved items, manage subscriptions through Apple, and request account or data deletion by contacting us.

13. California Privacy Notice

We do not sell your personal information. We do not knowingly share your personal information for cross-context behavioral advertising. California users may request access, correction, or deletion of personal information by contacting us.

14. European Privacy Rights

If you are located in the European Economic Area, United Kingdom, or Switzerland, you may have the right to request access, correction, deletion, restriction, portability, or objection to certain processing of your personal data. To exercise these rights, contact us.

15. Children’s Privacy

MacroChef is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

16. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we may notify you in the app or by other reasonable means.

17. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or want to request account/data deletion, contact us at: [macrocheffounder@gmail.com]
