Privacy Policy for MacroChef

Last updated: April 10, 2026

MacroChef (“we,” “our,” or “us”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how MacroChef collects, uses, stores, and protects information when you use the MacroChef mobile app.

By using MacroChef, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect information you provide directly in the app, including:

Account Information

  • Email address
  • Username or user ID
  • Authentication provider information if you sign in with a third-party provider

Profile and Goal Information

  • Age
  • Sex/gender selection
  • Height
  • Current weight
  • Goal weight
  • Activity level
  • Fitness or nutrition goal
  • Measurement preferences
  • Daily nutrition targets

Nutrition and App Usage Data

  • Food logs
  • Macro and calorie entries
  • Weight logs
  • Generated meals
  • Saved meals
  • Favorite meals
  • Grocery list items
  • Kitchen or pantry items
  • Allergy and dietary preference settings

Photos and Scans

If you use photo-based features, MacroChef may process photos you submit for food photo analysis, barcode scanning, or kitchen/fridge scanning. These photos are used to provide the requested feature, such as estimating nutrition, detecting barcode information, or suggesting visible kitchen ingredients.

Subscription Data

MacroChef uses Apple and RevenueCat to manage subscriptions. We may receive subscription status information, such as whether you have active Pro access, but we do not receive or store your full payment card details.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Create and manage your account
  • Personalize calorie and macro targets
  • Generate meal recommendations
  • Suggest fast-food orders based on your goals
  • Analyze food photos and kitchen photos
  • Maintain your food logs, grocery list, and saved meals
  • Sync your data across sessions
  • Provide subscription access
  • Improve app reliability and user experience
  • Provide support and troubleshoot issues

3. AI Features

MacroChef uses AI-powered features to help generate meals, estimate food nutrition, suggest fast-food orders, suggest ingredient swaps, and identify possible kitchen ingredients.

When you use these features, relevant inputs may be sent to AI service providers so the app can return a result. These inputs may include meal preferences, nutrition goals, selected foods or kitchen ingredients, restaurant choice, and submitted food or kitchen images.

AI results are estimates and suggestions. You should review generated meals, nutrition estimates, allergy-sensitive recommendations, and scan results before relying on them.

4. Third-Party Services

MacroChef may use third-party services to operate the app, including:

  • Supabase: authentication, account data, profile storage, food logs, saved meals, grocery lists, and data syncing.
  • RevenueCat: subscription status and premium access management.
  • Apple: App Store purchases, subscriptions, refunds, and payment processing.
  • OpenAI or other AI service providers: AI-powered meal generation, food photo analysis, fast-food recommendations, and kitchen scan suggestions.
  • Open Food Facts: packaged food information when barcode scanning is used.

These third-party services may process data according to their own privacy policies.

5. How We Store and Protect Data

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Your data may be stored using cloud services such as Supabase.

6. Allergies and Health Information

MacroChef may allow you to enter allergies, dietary preferences, weight, nutrition targets, and related information. This information is used to personalize recommendations.

MacroChef is not a medical app and does not provide medical advice. Always consult a qualified healthcare professional for medical, allergy, nutrition, or dietary advice.

7. Subscriptions and Payments

Payments are processed by Apple through the App Store. MacroChef does not collect or store your full credit card or payment card information.

Subscription management, cancellation, refunds, and billing are handled through Apple and your Apple ID subscription settings.

8. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information.

We may share information with service providers only as needed to operate the app, provide AI features, manage subscriptions, store user data, or comply with legal obligations.

9. Data Retention

We keep your information for as long as needed to provide MacroChef services, maintain your account, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements.

You may request deletion of your account or data by contacting us.

10. Your Choices

You may:

  • Update profile information in the app
  • Change allergy and dietary preferences
  • Delete or modify certain logs and saved items
  • Manage or cancel subscriptions through Apple
  • Request account or data deletion by contacting us

11. California Privacy Notice

We do not sell your personal information. We do not knowingly share your personal information for cross-context behavioral advertising.

California users may request access, correction, or deletion of personal information by contacting us.

12. European Privacy Rights

If you are located in the European Economic Area, United Kingdom, or Switzerland, you may have the right to request access, correction, deletion, restriction, portability, or objection to certain processing of your personal data.

To exercise these rights, contact us.

13. Children’s Privacy

MacroChef is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

14. International Users

If you use MacroChef outside the United States, your information may be processed in countries where our service providers operate. By using the app, you consent to that processing.

15. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we may notify you in the app or by other reasonable means.
